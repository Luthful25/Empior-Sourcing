Core Services

Structured support across the full sourcing cycle.

Each service area is designed to reduce friction for buyers while improving visibility, quality, and delivery confidence.

01

Product Development

We coordinate proto, fit, salesman, and pre-production samples with disciplined revision management and buyer approval follow-up.

  • Tech pack review and development planning
  • Sample tracking and revision control
  • Fabric, trim, lab dip, and print approval support

02

Factory Sourcing

We match each order with the right factory based on product type, capacity, compliance, pricing, and delivery expectations.

  • Shortlisting aligned manufacturing partners
  • Capability evaluation and compliance review
  • Balanced sourcing for price and performance

03

Costing And Negotiation

We prepare practical costing routes and help buyers reach workable targets without compromising quality or timeline stability.

  • Cost breakdown analysis
  • Price alignment with factory partners
  • Commercial support during booking confirmation

04

Production Follow-Up

Our team monitors raw materials, line planning, output status, and critical path milestones to keep execution on track.

  • Time and action calendar management
  • Material and production status reporting
  • Early escalation of risks or delays

05

Quality Assurance

Quality checks are built into the process through inline monitoring, pre-final review, and shipment-readiness confirmation.

  • Inline and final inspection coordination
  • Measurement, workmanship, and packing checks
  • Corrective action follow-up before shipment

06

Shipment Coordination

We support the final handover with documentation checks, booking readiness, and communication between factory, buyer, and forwarder.

  • Packing list and document review
  • Shipment booking and handover readiness
  • Final status updates before dispatch
