About Us

Building an empire of purpose through responsible sourcing, disciplined execution, and long-term trust.

Empior Sourcing was established with a clear mandate: to redefine what global brands should expect from a Bangladeshi sourcing partner. In an industry often fragmented by communication gaps, quality inconsistencies, and transactional relationships, we chose a different path.

Who We Are

A Bangladesh-based sourcing partner built for modern buyers who need less noise and more control.

Empior Sourcing is a premium apparel sourcing company headquartered in Bangladesh, the world's second-largest ready-made garment exporter. Founded in 2025, we operate at the intersection of manufacturing excellence and global brand requirements—delivering not just products, but complete supply chain solutions. We are not middlemen. We are your dedicated presence on the factory floor, your quality assurance system, and your compliance management partner. From the first design sketch to the final container dispatch, we manage every critical touchpoint with precision, transparency, and accountability. Our identity is rooted in Bangladesh's RMG revolution. We have grown alongside this industry, witnessed its transformation, and now serve as custodians of its future. Every order we manage carries the weight of millions of livelihoods, decades of progress, and the promise of what Bangladesh can become. We are Empior Sourcing. We deliver what the world demands—exactly as promised, every single time.

Our Foundation

Our journey began not in corporate boardrooms, but in the manufacturing zones of Gazipur and Dhaka. We observed firsthand how Bangladeshi factories were evolving—implementing water recycling systems, reducing chemical usage, and achieving green certifications. We recognized that the world needed to see this transformation, not the outdated stereotypes.

OUR STORY

I am Mahmudul Hasan Rounok, Managing Director of Empior Sourcing. Like many in this industry, my story is inseparable from the story of Bangladesh’s Ready-Made Garment (RMG) sector. I grew up watching its quiet revolution — not in headlines, but in human lives. I have seen how a single purchase order can ripple through generations. How a worker in Gazipur earns not just wages, but dignity — sending her children to school, shaping futures beyond factory walls. How small workshops, stitched together by resilience, grow into enterprises that stand on the global stage.

From modest beginnings in the late 20th century, Bangladesh has risen to become the second-largest apparel exporter in the world. Today, the RMG sector is not merely an industry — it is the backbone of our economy, the lifeline of millions, and a testament to what determination can achieve. This is not just business for us. This is our story. Our identity. Our future.
